House Description

English | 中文

PURe+ house takes its form from traditional courtyard houses that have been common throughout East Asia. A central courtyard can bring a variety of functional benefits such as ample daylight exposure and external view that each wing of the house could have, and fresh air through natural ventilation deep into the house. In addition, PURe+ house centered by a courtyard keeps an architectural form of vernacular houses that have stood in East Asia for centuries.

PURe+ house will be equipped with contemporary building materials, wired/wireless sensors and cutting-edge building technologies including dynamic building envelopes responsive to time-varying indoor and outdoor environment, smart shading devices, active PV panels, Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) heat pump for heating and cooling, Energy Recovery Ventilator (ERV), radiant wall panel, etc. All aforementioned technologies have been virtually tested and optimally designed with advanced building simulation tools. The tools and methods used for the PURe+ house design include the following:

  • PHPP 2007 (ISO 13790 based): to estimate heating and cooling demand (monthly, annual) of PURe+ house
  • Therm6: to analyze heat bridge phenomena in building envelopes
  • RETScreen: to analyze solar radiation and energy production of PV system
  • EnergyPlus & MATLAB: sensitivity and uncertainty analysis for design variables (e.g., insulation thickness, U value and g-value of windows, lighting)

To make PURe+ house market-viable, modular construction was introduced. Modular construction is an industrialized construction system, highlighted by efficient factory production of pre-engineered building units and a quick delivery of fabricated units to a construction site and fast assembly. Each module, a size of 3.580 (m) by 1.780 (m), includes the floor, walls and ceiling/roof with pre-installed plumbing and electrical systems, and interior and exterior finishes.

PURe+ house的形式来自东亚共有的传统庭院形式。中央庭院可以带来各种功能优势，例如充足的日光照射和外部景观，还有新鲜空气通过自然通风进入到房子的深处。此外，以庭院为中心的PURe+ house 保持了几个世纪以来在东亚的传统民居建筑形式。

PURe+ house 将配备现代建材、有线/无线传感器和先进的建筑技术，例如动态围护结构响应随时间变化的室内和室外环境，智能遮阳设备，活跃的光伏电池板，变制冷剂流量（VRF）加热和冷却的热泵，热回收装置（ERV），辐射墙面板等。所有上述技术已经通过先进的建筑模拟工具进行了虚拟测试和最佳设计。用于 PURe+ house 设计的工具和方法包括以下内容：

  • PHPP 2007（基于ISO 13790）：估算PURe+ house的供暖和制冷需求（每月，每年）
  • Therm6：分析建筑围护结构的热桥现象
  • RETScreen：分析太阳辐射和光伏系统的能源生产
  • EnergyPlus ＆ MATLAB：设计变量的敏感度和不确定性分析（例如，绝缘厚度，窗户的U值和g值，照明）

为了PURe+ house市场的可行性，引入了模块化建设。模块化建设是一个工业化的建筑系统，突出的是高效的工厂生产能力，如预先设计的建筑单元和快速交付制造单位到建筑工地和快速组装。每个模块，大小为3.580（M）和1.780（M），这里包括地板，墙壁和天花板/屋顶与预先安装的管道和电气系统，和内部和外部装饰。
